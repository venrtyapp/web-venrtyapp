FAQ

Reception Management Software - Questions Answered

What is reception management software?

Reception management software automates the visitor-related tasks handled by your front desk - logging visitors, notifying hosts, printing badges, managing pre-registered visitors, and maintaining security. It frees up your receptionist to focus on providing a great welcome experience.

Can Ventryx work alongside a human receptionist?

Yes. Ventryx complements your receptionist, not replaces them. The receptionist checks in visitors from their desktop dashboard, sees all expected visitors, sends invites, and generates reports - all within Ventryx. Visitors can also self-check-in on the kiosk.

Does reception management software work for multi-reception offices?

Yes. Each reception or entry point has its own kiosk, but all visitor data is centralised in a single dashboard. Security managers see activity from all receptions simultaneously, and reports aggregate data across all entry points.

What happens when the receptionist is away or at lunch?

During unattended periods, visitors self-check-in on the kiosk tablet. Hosts are notified automatically. No visitor waits at an empty desk - the system handles the initial interaction and the host is alerted to come to reception.

How does reception management software handle expected vs walk-in visitors?

Expected visitors are pre-registered with a digital invite - check-in on arrival takes 10 seconds. Walk-in visitors check in on the kiosk by entering their name, purpose, and host. The receptionist dashboard shows both types clearly each morning.
