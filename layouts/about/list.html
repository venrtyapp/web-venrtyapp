Our Mission

Making Indian Workplaces Safer and Smarter

We started Ventryx after visiting hundreds of Indian offices and watching the same scene play out every day - a visitor walks in, a receptionist reaches for a paper register, scribbles a name, calls a host who doesn't pick up, and the visitor waits awkwardly in the lobby.

We knew there was a better way. And we knew that the "better way" couldn't be a product designed for Silicon Valley offices - it had to be built from scratch for Indian workplaces: intermittent internet, WhatsApp-first communication, multi-language teams, and compliance needs specific to India.

That's Ventryx. Purpose-built for India.
