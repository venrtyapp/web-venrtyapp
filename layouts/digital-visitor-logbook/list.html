Everything a Paper Register Can't Do

Ventry's digital visitor log captures more data, protects visitor privacy, and makes every audit instant - without any manual effort.

Instant Search

Find any visitor record in seconds - search by name, company, date, host, purpose, or any custom field. No manual page-flipping.

Tamper-Proof Records

Every entry is server-timestamped with a full audit trail. Unlike paper registers, digital logs in Ventry cannot be altered or destroyed.

Photo at Check-in

Kiosk camera captures visitor photo at entry. Every log record has a face attached - eliminating the "John Doe, Google" problem of paper registers.

One-Click Audit Export

Export visitor logs as PDF or Excel for any date range - instantly. Audit-ready in seconds, not hours of manual preparation.

Each visitor sees only their own entry - not everyone else's. Unlike paper registers where your entire client list is visible to every new visitor.

Records backed up in real time to India servers (Mumbai AWS). A fire, flood, or office move cannot destroy years of visitor records.

Full Visit Timeline

Every record shows check-in time, check-out time, total duration, and purpose - all automatically captured without manual entry.

Custom Fields

Add custom fields to your log: vehicle number, NDA signed, PPE compliance, ID document number - whatever your compliance requires.
