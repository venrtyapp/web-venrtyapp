FAQ

Visitor Management Software - Questions Answered

What is visitor management software?

Visitor management software is a digital system that replaces paper visitor registers at offices and workplaces. It captures visitor information, notifies hosts, prints visitor badges, and maintains a searchable log of all visits. Modern visitor management software like Ventryx also includes QR code check-in, watchlist screening, and compliance reports.

How much does visitor management software cost in India?

Ventryx visitor management software starts at ₹999/month for up to 25 employees on the Starter plan. The Professional plan at ₹2,999/month supports unlimited employees and includes badge printing, watchlist screening, and multi-location management. All plans include a 14-day free trial.

Is visitor management software PDPB compliant?

Ventryx stores all visitor data on servers in India (Mumbai AWS region) and is aligned with India's Personal Data Protection Bill (PDPB). Visitor data is encrypted, access-controlled, and never shared with third parties.

Can visitor management software work without internet?

Yes. Ventryx has an offline mode for the kiosk app. Visitor check-ins are stored locally and synced to the cloud when connectivity is restored - critical for Indian offices with intermittent internet.

What hardware do I need to run visitor management software?

All you need is a standard Android or iPad tablet (7 inch or larger) placed on a stand at your reception. Optionally, connect a Zebra or Dymo label printer for automatic badge printing. No servers, no special hardware. Total setup cost under ₹15,000.
