Shift management software helps businesses create, assign, and track employee work shifts. It automates scheduling, calculates overtime based on shift rules, sends shift reminders to employees, and tracks compliance with shift assignments. Ventry shift management works for any type of schedule - fixed, flexible, or rotational.

Yes. Ventry supports automated rotational shift scheduling. You define the rotation pattern - for example, Week A on Morning shift, Week B on Afternoon, Week C on Night - and the system automatically assigns the correct shift to each employee for each week. Managers just review and approve the generated roster.

Overtime is calculated automatically based on the rules you define for each shift. If a Morning shift is 8 hours and an employee works 10 hours, Ventry records 2 hours overtime. You can set overtime thresholds, approval requirements, and maximum overtime limits per week or month - all configurable per shift type.
