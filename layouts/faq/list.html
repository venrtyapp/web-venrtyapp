Visitor Management System

Questions about how Ventry handles visitors, kiosk setup, and security at your premises.

A Visitor Management System (VMS) automates the visitor check-in process at your premises. Here's how Ventry works:

  • Before visit: The host sends a digital invite with a QR code.
  • At entry: The visitor scans the QR or checks in on a tablet kiosk — filling in name, purpose, and uploading ID if required.
  • Notification: The host gets an SMS/WhatsApp/email alert instantly.
  • During visit: Visitor details are live in the dashboard — visible to security and the host.
  • Exit: Visitor checks out. The log is updated with total visit duration.

Any Android tablet (7 inch or larger) or iPad works as a kiosk. For badge printing, you need a compatible label printer (Zebra ZD220, Dymo LabelWriter, or similar). We can recommend hardware based on your budget — good setups start from under ₹15,000.

Yes. The kiosk camera captures a photo at check-in. This is optional but highly recommended for security. Photos are stored securely and only accessible to authorised admin users.

You define shifts (Morning, Afternoon, Night, etc.) with start/end times and grace periods. Employees are assigned to shifts individually or by department. For rotational shifts, you set the rotation schedule and Ventry automatically assigns the correct shift each day. Overtime is calculated based on the assigned shift's rules.

We have native integrations with Razorpay Payroll, GreytHR, and Keka. For all other payroll software, you can export reports in Excel/CSV in the exact format your payroll team needs. REST API is available for Enterprise custom integrations.
