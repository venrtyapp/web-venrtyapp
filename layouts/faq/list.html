Visitor Management System

What is a Visitor Management System and how does it work?

A Visitor Management System (VMS) is software that automates the visitor check-in process at your premises. Here's how it works with Ventryx:

  • Before visit: The host sends a digital invite with a QR code.
  • At entry: The visitor scans the QR code or checks in on a tablet kiosk - filling in their name, purpose, and uploading ID if required.
  • Notification: The host gets an SMS/WhatsApp/email alert instantly.
  • During visit: The visitor's details are live in the system - visible to security and the host.
  • Exit: The visitor checks out. The log is updated with total duration.

Does Ventryx VMS work offline (without internet)?

Yes. The Ventryx kiosk app has an offline mode - it stores check-in data locally on the device and automatically syncs to the cloud when connectivity is restored. This is critical for Indian offices where internet can be intermittent.

What hardware do I need for the visitor kiosk?

Any Android tablet (7 inch or larger) or iPad works as a kiosk. For badge printing, you need a compatible label printer (Zebra ZD220, Dymo LabelWriter, or similar). We can recommend hardware based on your budget - good setups start from under ₹15,000.

Can I capture visitor photos at check-in?

Yes. The kiosk camera captures a photo at check-in. This is optional but highly recommended for security. Photos are stored securely and only accessible to authorised admin users.

How does visitor watchlist screening work?

You maintain a list of names or ID numbers to block. During check-in, the visitor's name is automatically checked against this list. If matched, the check-in is blocked and a real-time alert is sent to designated security staff. The visitor sees a neutral message to contact the front desk - they are not informed of the match reason.

Is Ventryx compliant with India's data protection laws?

Yes. All data is stored in India (Mumbai AWS region). We're aligned with the PDPB (Personal Data Protection Bill) - visitors can request deletion of their data, and we provide full data portability to customers. Enterprise customers receive a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

Attendance Management System

Can employees mark attendance from their mobile phones?

Yes. The Ventryx mobile app (Android and iOS) allows employees to check in and out with one tap. For verification, you can require GPS location proof, a selfie, or restrict check-ins to within a defined geofence around your office. The app works even in low-network conditions.

Do I need to replace my biometric device?

Not necessarily. Ventryx works standalone without biometric hardware. If you want to continue using your existing biometric device (ZKTeco, eSSL, Matrix), we have integration support to sync data from those devices into Ventryx for centralised reporting.

How does shift management work?

You define shifts (Morning, Afternoon, Night, etc.) with start/end times and grace periods. Employees are assigned to shifts individually or by department. For rotational shifts, you set the rotation schedule and Ventryx automatically assigns the correct shift each day. Overtime is calculated based on the assigned shift's rules.

Which payroll software does Ventryx integrate with?

We have native integrations with Razorpay Payroll, GreytHR, and Keka. For all other payroll software, you can export reports in Excel/CSV in the exact format your payroll team needs. REST API is available for Enterprise custom integrations.

Can employees regularise missed attendance?

Yes. Employees submit a regularisation request with a reason. It goes through the manager approval workflow. All regularisations are logged separately in reports - HR has full visibility into how many regularisations were approved/rejected per employee.
