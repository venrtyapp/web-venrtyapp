Four Ways to Check In - All Under 60 Seconds

Ventry adapts to every visitor type and office setup - from pre-registered VIP clients to unannounced walk-in visitors.

QR Code Self Check-in

A QR code displayed on the kiosk tablet. Visitors scan with their phone - a branded web form opens instantly. No app, no account needed. Done in 30 seconds.

Pre-Registration Link

Host sends an invite link before the visit. Visitor fills details from home. On arrival, scan the QR and check in is complete in 10 seconds - fastest method.

Kiosk Self Check-in

Fully self-service tablet kiosk at your entrance. Visitors tap the touchscreen to complete check-in without any receptionist involvement.

Reception-Assisted Check-in

Your receptionist checks in visitors from the web dashboard - ideal for VIP guests, elderly visitors, or accessibility needs.

Instant Host Notification

The moment a visitor checks in, their host gets an instant WhatsApp, SMS, or email notification - no manual lobby calls, no waiting.

Auto Badge Printing

Visitor badge auto-prints on check-in - with photo, name, host, and expiry. No manual badge filling. Works with Zebra and Dymo printers.

Watchlist Auto-Screen

Every check-in is silently screened against your watchlist. Security receives an instant alert if a flagged visitor attempts entry.

Photo Capture at Entry

Kiosk camera captures visitor photo at check-in - stored with their visit record for visual verification and security review.
