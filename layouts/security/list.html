Encryption

Data Encrypted Everywhere

Every byte of visitor and employee data is encrypted - at rest in our databases and in transit over the network. We use industry-standard encryption that is mathematically impossible to brute-force with current technology.

At Rest - AES-256

All data stored in our databases and file storage is encrypted using AES-256 - the same standard used by banks and government agencies. Encryption keys are managed using AWS KMS with automatic rotation.

In Transit - TLS 1.3

All communication between your browser, our mobile apps, and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. We enforce HTTPS-only and use HSTS headers to prevent downgrade attacks.

Encryption at a glance

Data Residency

Your Data Stays in India

All Ventryx data is stored and processed exclusively on servers located in India. We do not transfer personal data outside India.

Mumbai AWS Region

Our primary infrastructure runs on AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai). All data - visitor logs, attendance records, employee profiles - is stored here.

Disaster Recovery in India

Our backup and disaster recovery site is also within India (Hyderabad region). Your data never leaves Indian soil - even for backups.

DPDP Act Compliant

Aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. We support data subject rights - access, correction, and erasure requests - within the statutory timelines.

Access Control

Who Can Access Your Data

We follow a strict least-privilege model. Only the people who need to see your data can see it - and every access is logged.

Role-Based Access Control

Define roles within your organisation - Admin, HR Manager, Security Officer, Receptionist. Each role sees only what it needs to. Custom roles available on Enterprise.

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA is available for all admin accounts via TOTP authenticator apps. Enterprise customers can enforce MFA organisation-wide and integrate with SSO (Google Workspace, Okta).

Full Audit Log

Every admin action - configuration changes, data exports, user additions - is logged with timestamp, IP address, and user identity. Audit logs are immutable and retained for 2 years.

Ventryx Employee Access

Our staff access your data only when required for support - always with your explicit request or consent. Access is time-limited, logged, and reviewed by our security team quarterly.

IP Allowlisting

Enterprise customers can restrict dashboard access to specific IP ranges - useful for organisations that want to prevent access from outside their office network or VPN.

Session Management

Inactive sessions are automatically expired. Admins can remotely terminate all active sessions for a user - useful when an employee leaves or a device is lost.
