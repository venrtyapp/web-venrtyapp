Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: June 1, 2026

Ventry Technologies Private Limited ("Ventry", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and their users. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account registration: name, email address, phone number, company name, designation
  • Payment information: billing address, GSTIN (processed by PCI-DSS compliant payment processors - we do not store card numbers)
  • Visitor data entered by customers: visitor names, contact details, ID documents, photos
  • Employee data: name, employee ID, department, shift, leave records

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser information (for app performance and security)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage logs and feature interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Ventry platform
  • To process payments and issue invoices
  • To send service notifications and support communications
  • To improve product features based on aggregate usage analytics
  • To comply with applicable laws including India's IT Act and PDPB

3. Data Storage and Security

All customer data is stored on servers located in India (Mumbai, AWS ap-south-1 region). We implement AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Access to customer data by Ventry staff is restricted on a need-to-know basis with full audit logging.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Infrastructure providers (AWS India) under data processing agreements
  • Payment processors (Razorpay) for payment handling
  • Communication services (Twilio for SMS) for notifications
  • Law enforcement when required by Indian law

5. Visitor Data (Customer-Controlled)

Visitor and employee data entered by our customers is owned and controlled by the customer. We process this data on behalf of customers under their privacy policies. If you are a visitor whose data was captured by a Ventry customer, contact that organisation directly to exercise your rights.

6. Data Retention

Active accounts: data retained for the duration of the subscription plus 90 days. Deleted accounts: data purged within 30 days unless legally required otherwise. Visitor log data: retained per customer's configured retention policy (default: 1 year).

7. Your Rights

Under India's IT Act and applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email privacy@venrtyapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies (anonymised, no cross-site tracking). See our Cookie Policy for details. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email to account holders at least 14 days before taking effect.

10. Contact Us

For privacy enquiries:
Email: privacy@venrtyapp.com
