Contractor & Vendor Gate Management

Third-party contractors, raw material suppliers, and equipment vendors check in at the main gate with ID verification and work permit logging.

Digital Safety Briefing at Entry

Contractors acknowledge safety rules and site-specific PPE requirements at the kiosk before entry. Digital signatures are stored for every visit - legal proof of compliance.

Multi-Shift Attendance

Manage morning, afternoon, and night shifts with automatic shift assignment. Late arrivals, early exits, and no-shows are flagged to the shift supervisor in real time.

Biometric Integration

Integrate with existing ZKTeco, eSSL, or Matrix biometric devices on the shop floor. Attendance data syncs automatically into Ventryx for centralised reporting.

Labour Law Compliance Reports

Auto-generate Form 25 (muster roll), overtime statements, and monthly wage registers required under the Factories Act and Shops & Establishments Act.

Visitor Escort Management

Buyers, auditors, and VIP visitors are assigned an internal escort at check-in. The escort receives a mobile alert and the visitor's location is tracked throughout the plant visit.

Zone-Based Access Control

Define restricted zones (production floor, chemical storage, server room). Visitors and contractors can only enter zones they're authorised for - enforced at gate level.

Vehicle Entry & Exit Log

Log vehicle numbers, material inward/outward details, and driver credentials for every truck or vehicle entering the plant. Integrates with weighbridge data.

FAQs

Common Questions from
Manufacturing

How does Ventryx handle daily wage and contract labour attendance?

Ventryx supports separate attendance tracking for permanent employees, contract workers, and daily wage labour. Each category can have different rules for overtime, grace periods, and leave. Monthly reports are generated in the format required by contractors and the Factories Act.

Can Ventryx manage attendance for workers without smartphones?

Yes. Workers without smartphones use the biometric kiosk or RFID card reader at the gate. Ventryx integrates with hardware from ZKTeco, eSSL, Matrix, and others - so your existing devices can be retained.

How do safety briefing acknowledgements work for contractors?

When a contractor checks in for the first time (or at configurable intervals like monthly), the kiosk displays your safety briefing video or slides. The contractor must tap 'I acknowledge' before check-in is completed. The timestamp and contractor ID are stored - this constitutes a legal digital record.

Does Ventryx support multiple plant locations under one account?

Yes. Enterprise customers manage multiple plant locations from a single dashboard. Each plant has its own admin, rules, and reports, but the top-level account gets a consolidated view across all plants.
