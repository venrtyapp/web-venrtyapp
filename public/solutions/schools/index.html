Key Capabilities

Everything You Need for
Schools

Purpose-built features for your industry - not a generic tool retrofitted to your needs.

Parent & Guardian Verification

Verify parents at the gate with photo ID and authorised pickup lists. Only approved guardians can collect students - every pickup is logged with timestamp and photo.

Staff Attendance Management

Teachers and non-teaching staff mark attendance with the mobile app or biometric kiosk. Late arrivals, early exits, and absences are flagged to the principal automatically.

Vendor & Contractor Check-In

Canteen vendors, maintenance contractors, and delivery personnel check in through a separate flow with mandatory ID upload and purpose logging.

Emergency Headcount

During a fire drill or emergency, one tap shows a live headcount of everyone on campus - students, staff, and visitors. Drill reports are generated automatically.

Watchlist Screening

Maintain a list of persons restricted from campus. Anyone attempting check-in is automatically screened and blocked if on the list - security is alerted immediately.

Digital Gate Register

Completely replace the paper gate register. Every entry and exit is time-stamped and stored digitally. Searchable and exportable for school management and audits.

Visitor Pre-Registration

Teachers or admin staff can pre-register expected visitors - parents for PTMs, auditors, inspectors - who get a QR code for fast contactless entry.

Multi-Gate Management

Manage main gate, side entrance, and staff entry separately from one dashboard. Different check-in rules for different gates.
