What's New in Ventry

Every feature we ship, every bug we fix, every improvement we make - documented here. We update this page with every release.

All systems operational Subscribe to release notes
v2.8.0 June 2026

AI-Powered Visitor Insights & DPDP Act Compliance Tools

New Enterprise
  • AI Visit Analytics: Automatically detect peak visitor hours, busiest hosts, and recurring visitor patterns. Available on Professional and Enterprise plans.
  • DPDP Act Compliance Dashboard: New privacy management panel - track visitor data requests, manage consent records, and handle right-to-erasure requests as required by India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.
  • Bulk Visitor Pre-Registration: Upload a CSV to pre-register hundreds of visitors for large events or conference days in one go.
  • WhatsApp Check-In Notifications: Hosts now receive WhatsApp messages (in addition to SMS/email) when their visitor arrives - using our verified business number.
  • Attendance: Compensatory Leave (Comp-Off) Module: Employees who work on holidays can now apply for comp-off directly from the mobile app. Manager approval workflow included.
v2.7.0 May 2026

Kiosk 2.0, Face Recognition & Payroll Integrations

New Improved
  • Kiosk App 2.0: Rebuilt visitor kiosk with faster QR scan (under 1 second), support for Android 14, and a new accessible design for reduced-vision users.
  • Face Recognition Check-In (Beta): Employees can now check in using face recognition on supported Android kiosks - no card or phone needed. Beta available on request.
  • Keka HR Integration: Native two-way sync with Keka - employee master data syncs from Keka, and attendance data flows back for payroll processing.
  • Multi-Language Kiosk: Visitor kiosk now supports Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, and Bengali in addition to English - configurable per location.
  • Fixed: Late mark calculation was incorrect for employees with custom shift grace periods. This is now resolved.
v2.6.0 April 2026

Coworking Module, Desk Booking & Guest WiFi

New
  • Coworking & Hot-Desk Module: Full hot-desk booking, real-time occupancy view, and multi-tenant visitor management for coworking spaces. Available as an add-on.
  • Guest WiFi Auto-Provisioning: On visitor check-in, a time-limited WiFi access code is automatically generated and sent to the visitor's phone via SMS.
  • Visitor Satisfaction Survey: Optionally send a one-question satisfaction survey to visitors via SMS after check-out. View ratings in the dashboard.
  • Improved Badge Designer: Drag-and-drop badge template editor - add your logo, choose fonts, and arrange fields without any coding.
v2.5.0 March 2026

Vehicle Log, Manufacturing Module & Overtime Rules

New Improved
  • Vehicle Entry Log: Log vehicle numbers, material details, and driver credentials at manufacturing plant gates. Searchable and exportable.
  • Digital Safety Briefing: Contractors must acknowledge safety rules on the kiosk before check-in is completed. Digital signature stored per visit.
  • Overtime Calculation Engine: Configurable overtime rules per shift - daily overtime, weekly overtime, and holiday premium rates. Factories Act compliant.
  • Attendance Regularisation Workflow: Employees submit missed attendance requests from the app - manager approval with full audit trail.
  • Fixed: Dashboard visitor count was not refreshing in real time on Safari browsers. Resolved.
v2.4.0 February 2026

School Safety Module, Watchlist Enhancements

New Fixed
  • Authorised Pickup List: Schools can configure an authorised guardian list per student. Only approved guardians can check out a student.
  • Emergency Headcount Report: One-tap report showing all visitors and staff currently on campus - designed for fire drills and emergency evacuations.
  • Watchlist - Photo Matching: Upload a photo to the watchlist. The system compares visitor check-in photos against the watchlist using visual similarity - flagging potential matches for security review.
  • Fixed: Visitor check-in email notifications were delayed by up to 4 minutes in some cases. Reduced to under 10 seconds.
v2.3.0 January 2026

Hospital Visitor Scheduling, NABH Report Templates

New Enterprise
  • Patient Visitor Scheduling: Hospitals can configure visiting hours and maximum visitor slots per patient per day. Families book slots in advance to avoid ward overcrowding.
  • NABH Compliance Report Templates: Pre-built report formats for NABH accreditation - visitor log summary, staff attendance with department breakdown, and infection screening log.
  • Infection Control Screening at Kiosk: Configurable health screening questionnaire at visitor check-in. Risk flags are sent to the infection control officer in real time.
  • GreytHR Payroll Integration: Native integration with GreytHR - attendance data syncs daily for payroll processing without manual exports.
v2.2.0 December 2025

Mobile App 2.0, Leave Module, Razorpay Payroll Sync

New Improved
  • Mobile App 2.0 (Android & iOS): Completely redesigned app - faster, more intuitive, with a new home screen showing today's attendance status, pending approvals, and upcoming visitors at a glance.
  • Leave Management Module: Employees apply for leave from the app, managers approve on mobile, and HR sees leave balances and reports - all within Ventry.
  • Razorpay Payroll Integration: Attendance and leave data syncs with Razorpay Payroll for automated payroll computation.
  • Dark Mode: The Ventry dashboard now supports dark mode - toggle it from your profile settings.
v2.1.0 November 2025

Multi-Location Support, API v2, ZKTeco Integration

New Enterprise
  • Multi-Location Dashboard: Enterprise customers managing multiple offices, plants, or campuses can now see a consolidated real-time view across all locations.
  • REST API v2: Completely revamped API with webhook support, improved rate limits, and new endpoints for visitor management, attendance, and user management.
  • ZKTeco Biometric Integration: Pull attendance punches from ZKTeco devices automatically into Ventry every 15 minutes.
  • Geofenced Attendance: Define a geofence radius around your office. Employee check-ins outside the boundary are flagged or blocked - configurable per location.
