FAQ

Digital Visitor Log Book - Questions Answered

What is a digital visitor log book?

A digital visitor log book is a software system that replaces the traditional paper visitor register. Visitors check in digitally - their information is stored in a searchable, secure database with timestamps, photos, and purpose of visit.

Is a digital visitor log book acceptable for audits and compliance?

Yes. Ventryx digital visitor logs are valid for ISO 27001 audits, SEBI compliance, factory inspections, and security audits. All records are timestamped, tamper-proof, and exportable as PDF or Excel. Auditors prefer digital logs because they're searchable and cannot be altered.

How long are visitor records stored in Ventryx?

By default, Ventryx stores visitor records for 1 year. Enterprise customers can configure retention from 6 months to 7 years. All data is stored in India on Mumbai AWS servers.

Can I search old visitor records?

Yes. Search by visitor name, company, host, date range, purpose of visit, or any custom field. Finding a specific visitor from 3 months ago takes seconds - not hours of manual register-flipping.

Is the digital visitor log tamper-proof?

Yes. Every entry carries a server-side timestamp and audit trail. You can see who created, edited, or deleted any record. Unlike a paper register, digital logs in Ventryx are cryptographically timestamped and backed up in real time.
