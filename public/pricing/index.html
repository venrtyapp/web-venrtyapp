Pricing Questions Answered

Is the pricing in Indian Rupees (INR)?

Yes. All Ventryx plans are priced in INR. Payments are accepted via UPI, NEFT, credit/debit cards, and we raise GST invoices for all subscriptions.

Can I switch plans after I start?

Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Professional at any time - the price difference is prorated for the remaining billing period. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do I get a GST invoice?

Yes. We issue a proper GST invoice for every payment. You can add your company GSTIN during checkout for input tax credit.

What happens after my free trial ends?

After 14 days, your account moves to a read-only mode - you can still view your data but cannot add new entries. No charges are applied automatically. You choose a plan and pay only if you want to continue.
