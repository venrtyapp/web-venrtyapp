Features

Everything You Need to Manage Attendance

From mobile check-in to payroll export - Ventry covers the complete attendance lifecycle for Indian businesses.

Mobile Check-In

One-tap check-in from Android or iOS. GPS location and optional selfie captured automatically for tamper-proof records.

GPS Geofencing

Restrict attendance marking to within a defined radius of your office or worksite. Works for multiple locations simultaneously.

Shift Management

Define unlimited shifts with custom timings, grace periods, and overtime rules. Automate rotational and night shift rosters.

Leave Management

Employees apply, managers approve, HR tracks - all in one platform. Supports EL, SL, CL, Comp Off, and custom leave types.

Overtime Tracking

Overtime calculated automatically against each shift's rules. Real-time alerts for managers when employees exceed thresholds.

Regularisation Workflow

Employees request regularisation for missed punches. Manager-approved and logged separately for full HR audit visibility.

Payroll-Ready Reports

One-click export of monthly attendance data to GreytHR, Keka, Razorpay Payroll, or any custom Excel/CSV format.

Kiosk Mode

Shared tablet at reception or factory gate for PIN or face-based check-in. No personal device required for every employee.

Attendance management software is a digital system that tracks when employees check in and out of work. Modern solutions like Ventry allow employees to mark attendance via mobile app, web browser, or kiosk, with GPS verification for remote and hybrid workers. The system calculates hours worked, overtime, late arrivals, and absences automatically.
