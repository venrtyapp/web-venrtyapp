Visitor Management

What is a Visitor Management System and why does my business need one?

A Visitor Management System (VMS) is a digital solution that replaces paper visitor registers at your reception. It captures visitor information (name, ID, photo), notifies hosts, prints badges, and maintains a searchable log of all visits.

Every Indian business that receives external visitors - clients, vendors, job candidates - needs a VMS for three reasons: security (know exactly who is on your premises), compliance (auditors require visitor logs), and professionalism (your reception is the first impression you make).

Does Ventryx work without an internet connection?

Ventryx is designed with India's connectivity in mind. The kiosk app has an offline mode - it stores check-in data locally and syncs to the cloud as soon as the connection is restored. So your front desk never goes down due to a network blip.

Can I customise the check-in form with my own fields?

Yes, completely. You can add custom fields like vehicle number, purpose of visit, department to visit, NDA signature, health declaration, or anything your company policy requires - all configured from your dashboard in minutes.

Attendance Management

Can employees mark attendance from their mobile phones?

Yes. The Ventryx mobile app (Android and iOS) lets employees check in and out with a single tap. For field teams or remote workers, it uses GPS to verify location at the time of check-in. You can restrict check-ins to a geofenced radius around office locations if needed.

Does Ventryx integrate with payroll software?

Ventryx exports payroll-ready attendance reports in formats compatible with all major Indian payroll systems. We have direct integrations with Razorpay Payroll, GreytHR, and Keka, and can export to Excel/CSV for any other system.

What happens if an employee forgets to check in?

Employees can submit a regularisation request for missed check-ins, which goes through the manager approval workflow. Managers can review and approve or reject these requests directly from their dashboard or the mobile app. All regularisations are logged for the audit trail.
