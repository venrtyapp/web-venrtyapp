1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide website owners with analytics information.

Cookies are not harmful - they do not contain viruses or malware. They cannot execute code or access files on your device. They can only be read by the website that placed them.

2. How Ventryx Uses Cookies

Ventryx uses cookies on two surfaces:

  • Marketing website (ventryx.com): We use cookies to understand how visitors find and use our website, to personalise content where relevant, and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
  • Ventryx application (app.ventryx.com): We use cookies to keep you logged in, to remember your dashboard preferences, and to ensure the application functions securely. These are strictly necessary and cannot be turned off without breaking the application.

5. India DPDP Act & Cookie Consent

India Data Protection

India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 governs how we handle personal data of Indian residents. Cookies that collect personal data (such as analytics cookies tied to identifiable users) require your consent. We obtain this through our cookie banner when you first visit our website.

Under the DPDP Act and the earlier PDPB framework, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent for optional cookies at any time
  • Request erasure of personal data associated with your cookie identifiers
  • Obtain a copy of data collected about your website visits

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@ventryx.com.

6. How to Manage Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

Cookie Preference Centre

Use our cookie banner (shown on first visit) to accept or decline optional cookies. You can change your preferences at any time.

Browser Settings

You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to the Ventryx application. Browser guides: Chrome, Firefox, Safari.

Google Analytics Opt-Out

Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent Google Analytics from collecting data on all websites you visit.
