FAQ

Leave Management Questions Answered

What is a leave management system?

A leave management system is software that automates employee leave requests, manager approvals, and leave balance tracking. Instead of WhatsApp messages or paper forms, employees apply for leave from an app, managers approve with one tap, and HR sees all leave records in one centralised platform with complete audit trails.

Does Ventryx support different types of leaves?

Yes. Ventryx supports all standard Indian leave types: Earned Leave (EL), Sick Leave (SL), Casual Leave (CL), Compensatory Off (Comp-Off), Maternity Leave, Paternity Leave, Optional Holiday, and Loss of Pay (LOP). You can also create any custom leave types your company policy requires, with specific accrual and carry-forward rules for each.

How does leave carry-forward work in Ventryx?

You configure carry-forward rules in Ventryx - how many days can be carried forward at year-end, maximum accumulation limits, and encashment policies. The system automatically applies these rules at the configured period end date. No manual HR intervention required.

Can employees check their leave balance on mobile?

Yes. Employees see their current leave balance for each leave type in the Ventryx mobile app. They can also see the status of pending leave requests, their approved leave history, and upcoming approved leaves. This eliminates the common HR query: "How many leaves do I have left?"

Is Ventryx leave management compliant with Indian labour law?

Yes. Ventryx's leave management is built to comply with India's Shops and Establishments Acts (which vary by state) and the Factories Act. Earned leave accrual rules are configurable per state, maternity leave follows the Maternity Benefit Act 1961 (26 weeks), and all approvals and rejections have a permanent audit trail for inspections.
