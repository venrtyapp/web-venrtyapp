Features

Everything Your Visitor Management Software Needs

Ventry covers every touchpoint of the visitor journey - from pre-registration to checkout.

QR Code Self Check-in

Visitors scan a QR code with their phone at reception. No app download, no waiting - check-in done in under 30 seconds.

Digital Pre-Registration

Employees send invite links to expected visitors. Data is captured before arrival - check-in on the day takes just 10 seconds.

Automatic Badge Printing

Branded visitor badges with photo, name, host, and validity auto-print on check-in. Works with Zebra and Dymo printers.

Watchlist Screening

Every visitor is automatically checked against your custom watchlist. Security gets an instant alert on any match - silently.

WhatsApp Host Notifications

Hosts receive instant WhatsApp, SMS, or email alerts the moment their visitor checks in. No more unanswered lobby calls.

Emergency Evacuation List

One-tap live evacuation list showing every person currently on premises - instantly shareable with emergency services.

Offline Mode

Check-ins work even without internet. Data syncs automatically when connectivity returns - built for Indian office realities.

Compliance Reports

Instant exportable visitor reports for ISO 27001 audits, SEBI inspections, and internal compliance. One click, audit-ready.

Visitor management software is a digital system that replaces paper visitor registers at offices and workplaces. It captures visitor information, notifies hosts, prints visitor badges, and maintains a searchable log of all visits. Modern visitor management software like Ventry also includes QR code check-in, watchlist screening, and compliance reports.
